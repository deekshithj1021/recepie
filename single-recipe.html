Chicken Biryani

Chicken biryani is a flavorful Indian dish made by marinating chicken in yogurt and spices, cooking fragrant Basmati rice with aromatic spices, and layering them with a rich gravy of onions, tomatoes, and spices. The layers are then cooked together on low heat until the flavors meld, creating a delicious and aromatic one-pot meal. Top it with saffron-infused milk, fried onions, and a drizzle of ghee for added richness, and serve with raita or salad for a complete and satisfying biryani experience.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Chicken Biryani Ghee Roast Chicken Tandoori Masala Fry

instructions

step 1

Ingredients: For Marinating Chicken: 500 grams of chicken, cut into pieces 1 cup yogurt 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste 1 teaspoon red chili powder 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder 1 teaspoon garam masala Salt to taste 2 tablespoons vegetable oil For Rice: 2 cups Basmati rice Water for soaking rice Water for cooking rice 1 bay leaf 2-3 green cardamom pods 2-3 cloves 1-inch cinnamon stick Salt to taste

step 2

For Biryani Gravy: 2 large onions, thinly sliced 2 tomatoes, chopped 2 green chilies, chopped 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 1 teaspoon cumin seeds 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder 1 teaspoon garam masala Salt to taste

step 3

For Layering: Saffron strands soaked in 2 tablespoons warm milk (for garnish) Ghee (clarified butter) for drizzling (optional)
